# 题目描述


<p>
<span style="font-weight:bold;font-size:10.5000pt;font-family:&#39;宋体&#39;;">【问题描述】</span><span style="font-weight:bold;font-size:10.5000pt;font-family:&#39;宋体&#39;;"></span> 
</p>
<p>
<span style="font-size:10.5000pt;font-family:&#39;宋体&#39;;"> </span><span style="font-size:10.5000pt;font-family:&#39;宋体&#39;;">尼克每天上班之前都连接上英特网，接收他的上司发来的邮件，这些邮件包含了尼克主管的部门当天要完成的全部任务，每个任务由一个开始时刻与一个持续时间构成。</span><span style="font-size:10.5000pt;font-family:&#39;宋体&#39;;"></span> 
</p>
<p>
<span style="font-size:10.5000pt;font-family:&#39;宋体&#39;;"> </span><span style="font-size:10.5000pt;font-family:&#39;宋体&#39;;">尼克的一个工作日为<span>N</span><span>分钟，从第一分钟开始到第</span><span>N</span><span>分钟结束。当尼克到达单位后他就开始干活。如果在同一时刻有多个任务需要完戍，尼克可以任选其中的一个来做，而其余的则由他的同事完成，反之如果只有一个任务，则该任务必需由尼克去完成，假如某些任务开始时刻尼克正在工作，则这些任务也由尼克的同事完成。如果某任务于第</span><span>P</span><span>分钟开始，持续时间为</span><span>T</span><span>分钟，则该任务将在第</span><span>P+T</span></span><span style="font-size:10.5000pt;font-family:&#39;宋体&#39;;">-</span><span style="font-size:10.5000pt;font-family:&#39;宋体&#39;;">1<span>分钟结束。</span></span><span style="font-size:10.5000pt;font-family:&#39;宋体&#39;;"></span> 
</p>
<p>
<span style="font-size:10.5000pt;font-family:&#39;宋体&#39;;"> </span><span style="font-size:10.5000pt;font-family:&#39;宋体&#39;;">写一个程序计算尼克应该如何选取任务，才能获得最大的空暇时间。</span><span style="font-size:10.5000pt;font-family:&#39;宋体&#39;;"></span> 
</p>
<p>
<span style="font-weight:bold;font-size:10.5000pt;font-family:&#39;宋体&#39;;">【输入】</span><span style="font-weight:bold;font-size:10.5000pt;font-family:&#39;宋体&#39;;"></span> 
</p>
<p>
<span style="font-size:10.5000pt;font-family:&#39;宋体&#39;;"> </span><span style="font-size:10.5000pt;font-family:&#39;宋体&#39;;">输入数据第一行含两个用空格隔开的整数<span>N</span><span>和</span><span>K</span></span><span style="font-size:10.5000pt;font-family:&#39;宋体&#39;;">(</span><span style="font-size:10.5000pt;font-family:&#39;宋体&#39;;">1<span>≤</span><span>N</span><span>≤</span><span>10000</span><span>，</span><span>1</span><span>≤</span><span>K</span><span>≤</span><span>10000</span></span><span style="font-size:10.5000pt;font-family:&#39;宋体&#39;;">)</span><span style="font-size:10.5000pt;font-family:&#39;宋体&#39;;">，<span>N</span><span>表示尼克的工作时间，单位为分钟，</span><span>K</span><span>表示任务总数。</span></span><span style="font-size:10.5000pt;font-family:&#39;宋体&#39;;"></span> 
</p>
<p>
<span style="font-size:10.5000pt;font-family:&#39;宋体&#39;;">    接下来共有<span>K</span><span>行，每一行有两个用空格隔开的整数</span><span>P</span><span>和</span><span>T</span><span>，表示该任务从第</span><span>P</span><span>分钟开始，持续时间为</span><span>T</span><span>分钟，其中</span><span>1</span><span>≤</span><span>P</span><span>≤</span><span>N</span><span>，</span><span>1</span><span>≤</span><span>P+T</span></span><span style="font-size:10.5000pt;font-family:&#39;宋体&#39;;">-</span><span style="font-size:10.5000pt;font-family:&#39;宋体&#39;;">1<span>≤</span><span>N</span><span>。</span></span><span style="font-size:10.5000pt;font-family:&#39;宋体&#39;;"></span> 
</p>
<p>
<span style="font-weight:bold;font-size:10.5000pt;font-family:&#39;宋体&#39;;">【输出】</span><span style="font-weight:bold;font-size:10.5000pt;font-family:&#39;宋体&#39;;"></span> 
</p>
<p>
<span style="font-size:10.5000pt;font-family:&#39;宋体&#39;;"> </span><span style="font-size:10.5000pt;font-family:&#39;宋体&#39;;">输出文件仅一行，包含一个整数，表示尼克可能获得的最大空暇时间。</span><span style="font-size:10.5000pt;font-family:&#39;宋体&#39;;"></span>
