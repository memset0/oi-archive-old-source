<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
题目描述
<small>Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

<p style=""><span style="">有n个房间以及m条双向通道。</span></p><p style=""><span style="">你需要找到有多少条从1号房间出发到n号房间路径，使得每个房间都恰好经过了一次。</span></p><p><br></p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
输入描述
<small>Input Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p style=""><span style="">第一行两个数n、m。</span></p><p style=""><span style="">接下来m行，每行两个数x、y，描述一条连接房间x和房间y的通道。</span></p><p style=""><span style="">保证每条通道连接两个不同的房间，并且每两个房间至多只有一条通道连接。</span></p><p><br></p>

</div>
</div>
<div  class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
输出描述
<small>Output Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

<p style="text-indent:28px"><span style="font-family:黑体">一行一个数，表示满足要求的路径条数。</span></p><p><br/></p>

</div>
</div>


<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
样例输入
<small>Sample Input</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p><span style="">5 7</span></p><p><span style="">1 2</span></p><p><span style="">1 3</span></p><p><span style="">3 2</span></p><p><span style="">3 4</span></p><p><span style="">1 5</span></p><p><span style="">5 4</span></p><p><span style="">4 2</span></p><p><br></p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
样例输出
<small>Sample Output</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p><span style="">2</span></p><p><br></p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
数据范围及提示
<small>Data Size & Hint</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p style=""><span style="">1</span><span style="">≤n≤10，0≤m≤n*(n-1)/2</span></p><p><br></p>
</div>
