题目描述
<small>Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

<p><span style="text-decoration: none;">对于给定的两个数n,r求</span><br></p><p><span style="text-decoration: none;">\sum_{d=1}^n (-1)^{\lfloor \sqrt {d \times r \times d} \rfloor}<br></span></p><p>(请将这个公式输入Latex显示)</p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
输入描述
<small>Input Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>第一行一个正整数T,为数据组数.<br></p><p>接下来T行,每行两个数n,r.</p>

</div>
</div>
<div  class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
输出描述
<small>Output Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

<p>对每组数据输出一行一个数为答案.<br/></p>

</div>
</div>
