<span>
题目描述
<small>Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

<p>(此题与队列练习1相比改了2处:1加强了数据 2不保证队空时不会出队)<br>给定一个队列(初始为空)，只有两种操作入队和出队，现给出这些操作请<br>输出最终的队头元素。 操作解释：1表示入队，2表示出队</p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
输入描述
<small>Input Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>N(操作个数)<br>N个操作(如果是入队则后面还会有一个入队元素)<br>具体见样例(输入保证队空时不会出队)</p>

</div>
</div>
<div  class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
输出描述
<small>Output Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

<p>最终队头元素，若最终队空，或队空时有出队操作，输出&rdquo;impossible!&rdquo;(不含引号)</p>

</div>
</div>


<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
样例输入
<small>Sample Input</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>3<br>1 2<br>2<br>2</p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
样例输出
<small>Sample Output</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>impossible!</p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
数据范围及提示
<small>Data Size & Hint</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>对于100%的数据  N≤100000 元素均为正整数且小于等于10^8</p>
</div>
