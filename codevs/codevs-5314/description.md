<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
题目描述
<small>Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

<p><span style="">蜗牛鲍勃在野外迷了路，想要找到它的爷爷和房子。它算清楚了需要经过n米到第一个寄宿点。而它的速度是x米一分钟。现在鲍勃想知道自己需要多少分钟才能到达寄宿点，如果到达寄宿点的时间超过一天，蜗牛鲍勃就没法找到寄宿点（no）。</span></p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
输入描述
<small>Input Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p><span style="">输入两个&gt;0的整数n和x，代表到寄宿点的距离n和速度x</span></p>

</div>
</div>
<div  class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
输出描述
<small>Output Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

<p><span style="font-family: 黑体, SimHei;">如果到达寄宿点的时间≤1天，输出要到达的分钟数。如果&gt;1天，输出“no”（不包括引号）。</span></p>

</div>
</div>


<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
样例输入
<small>Sample Input</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p><span style='font-family: "comic sans ms";'>10000 2</span></p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
样例输出
<small>Sample Output</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p><span style='font-family: "comic sans ms";'>no</span></p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
数据范围及提示
<small>Data Size & Hint</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p style=""><strong><span style="">数据范围：n和x在int范围内，x≤n.</span></strong></p><p style=""><strong><span style="">如果剩下的距离不够1分钟的速度，也按1分钟计算。</span></strong></p><p><br></p>
</div>
