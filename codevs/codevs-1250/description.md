题目描述
<small>Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

<p>定义：f0=f1=1, fn=fn-1+fn-2(n&gt;=2)。{fi}称为Fibonacci数列。</p>
<p>输入n，求fn mod q。其中1&lt;=q&lt;=30000。</p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
输入描述
<small>Input Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>第一行一个数T(1&lt;=T&lt;=10000)。</p>
<p>以下T行，每行两个数，n，q(n&lt;=10<sup>9</sup>, 1&lt;=q&lt;=30000)</p>

</div>
</div>
<div  class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
输出描述
<small>Output Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

<p class="p0">文件包含T行，每行对应一个答案。</p>

</div>
</div>


<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
样例输入
<small>Sample Input</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>3</p>
<p>6 2</p>
<p>7 3</p>
<p>7 11</p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
样例输出
<small>Sample Output</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>1</p>
<p>0</p>
<p>10</p>

</div>
</div>

<small>Data Size & Hint</small>
</span></div>
