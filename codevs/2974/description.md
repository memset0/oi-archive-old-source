<span>
题目描述
<small>Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

<p>和所有人一样，奶牛喜欢变化。它们正在设想新造型的牧场。奶牛建筑师Hei想建造围有漂亮白色栅栏的三角形牧场。她拥有N(3≤N≤40)块木板，每块的长度Li(1≤Li≤40)都是整数，她想用所有的木板围成一个三角形使得牧场面积最大。 <br>请帮助Hei小姐构造这样的牧场，并计算出这个最大牧场的面积。</p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
输入描述
<small>Input Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p><span>第1行：一个整数N </span><br><span>第2..N+1行：每行包含一个整数，即是木板长度。</span></p>

</div>
</div>
<div  class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
输出描述
<small>Output Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

<p><span>仅一个整数：最大牧场面积乘以100然后舍尾的结果。如果无法构建，输出-1。</span></p>

</div>
</div>


<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
样例输入
<small>Sample Input</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p><span>5</span><br><span>1</span><br><span>1</span><br><span>3</span><br><span>3</span><br><span>4</span></p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
样例输出
<small>Sample Output</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p><span>692</span></p>

</div>
</div>
