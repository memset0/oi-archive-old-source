<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
题目描述
<small>Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

<p>瑞瑞有一堆的玩具木棍，每根木棍的两端分别被染上了某种颜色，现在他突然有了一个想法，想要把这些木棍连在一起拼成一条线，并且使得木棍与木棍相接触的两端颜色都是相同的，给出每根木棍两端的颜色，请问是否存在满足要求的排列方式。</p>
<p>　　例如，如果只有<span style="font-family: Times New Roman;">2</span><span style="">根木棍，第一根两端的颜色分别为</span><span style="font-family: Times New Roman;">red,blue</span><span style="">，第二根两端的颜色分别为</span><span style="font-family: Times New Roman;">red,yellow</span><span style="">，那么</span><span style="font-family: Times New Roman;">blue---red|red----yellow</span><span style="">便是一种满足要求的排列方式。</span></p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
输入描述
<small>Input Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>输入有若干行，每行包括两个单词，表示一根木棍两端的颜色，单词由小写字母组成，且单词长度不会超过<span style="font-family: Times New Roman;">10</span><span style="">个字母，最多有</span><span style="font-family: Times New Roman;">250000</span><span style="">根木棍。</span></p>

</div>
</div>
<div  class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
输出描述
<small>Output Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

<p class="p0">如果木棒能够按要求排列，输出<span style="font-family: Calibri;">Possible</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">，否则输出</span><span style="font-family: Calibri;">Impossible</span></p>

</div>
</div>


<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
样例输入
<small>Sample Input</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>blue red</p>
<p>red violet</p>
<p>cyan blue</p>
<p>blue magenta</p>
<p>magenta cyan</p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
样例输出
<small>Sample Output</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>Possible</p>

</div>
</div>
