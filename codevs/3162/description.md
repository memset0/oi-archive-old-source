<div class="panel panel-default">

<span>
题目描述
<small>Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

<p>现在要把M本有顺序的书分给K个人复制（抄写），每一个人的抄写速度都一样，一本书不允许给两个（或以上）的人抄写，分给每一个人的书，必须是连续的，比如不能把第一、第三、第四本数给同一个人抄写。现在请你设计一种方案，使得复制时间最短。复制时间为抄写页数最多的人用去的时间。</p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
输入描述
<small>Input Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p style="">第一行两个整数<span style="font-family: Times New Roman,serif;"><span><span style="">M</span></span></span>、<span style="font-family: Times New Roman,serif;"><span><span style="">K</span></span></span>；（<span style="font-family: Times New Roman,serif;"><span><span style="">K&lt;=M&lt;=100</span></span></span>）</p>
<p style="">第二行<span style=""><span>M</span></span>个整数，第<span style=""><span>i</span></span>个整数表示第<span style=""><span>i</span></span>本书的页数。</p>

</div>
</div>
<div  class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
输出描述
<small>Output Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

<p class="cjk" style="text-indent: 0.73cm; margin-bottom: 0cm;">共<span style="font-family: 宋体,SimSun;"><span lang="en-US">K</span></span>行，每行两个正整数，第<span style="font-family: 宋体,SimSun;"><span lang="en-US">i</span></span>行表示第<span style="font-family: 宋体,SimSun;"><span lang="en-US">i</span></span>个人抄写的书的起始编号和终止编号。<span style="font-family: 宋体,SimSun;"><span lang="en-US">K</span></span>行的起始编号应该从小到大排列，如果有多解，则尽可能让前面的人少抄写。</p>

</div>
</div>


<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
样例输入
<small>Sample Input</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p style=""><span style=""><span>9 3</span></span></p>
<p style=""><span style=""><span>1 2 3 4 5 6 7 8 9</span></span></p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
样例输出
<small>Sample Output</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p style=""><span style=""><span>1 5</span></span></p>
<p style=""><span style=""><span>6 7</span></span></p>
<p style=""><span style=""><span>8 9</span></span></p>

</div>
</div>
