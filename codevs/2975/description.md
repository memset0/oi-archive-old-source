<span>
题目描述
<small>Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

<p>定义与2，3，5无关的数如下：</p>
<p>（1）位数不得是2，3，5的倍数；</p>
<p>（2）该数不能被2，3，5整除；</p>
<p>（3）该数必须为整数。</p>
<p>写一个判断一个数是否为与2，3，5无关的数的程序，如果有关，输出“No”，如果无关，输出“YeS”。</p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
输入描述
<small>Input Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>一行一个数。</p>

</div>
</div>
<div  class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
输出描述
<small>Output Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

<p>一行，有关输出&ldquo;No&rdquo;，无关输出&ldquo;YeS&rdquo;.[不含引号]</p>

</div>
</div>


<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
样例输入
<small>Sample Input</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>(样例1)13</p>
<p>(样例2)222</p>
<p>(样例3)999.9</p>
<p>(样例4)-7</p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
样例输出
<small>Sample Output</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>(样例1)No</p>
<p>(样例2)No</p>
<p>(样例3)No</p>
<p>(样例4)YeS</p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="area-title">
