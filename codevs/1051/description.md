<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
题目描述
<small>Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

<p>给出了<span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman';">N</span><span style="">个单词，已经按长度排好了序。如果某单词</span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman';">i</span><span style="">是某单词</span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman';">j</span><span style="">的前缀，</span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman';">i-&gt;j</span><span style="">算一次接龙(两个相同的单词不能算接龙)。</span></p>
<p>你的任务是：对于输入的单词，找出最长的龙。</p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
输入描述
<small>Input Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>第一行为<span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman';">N(1&lt;=N&lt;=10</span><sup>5</sup>)<span style="">。以下</span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman';">N</span><span style="">行每行一个单词（由小写组成），已经按长度排序。</span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman';">(</span><span style="">每个单词长度</span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman';">&lt;50)</span></p>

</div>
</div>
<div  class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
输出描述
<small>Output Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

<p class="p0">仅一个数，为最长的龙的长度。</p>

</div>
</div>


<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
样例输入
<small>Sample Input</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>5</p>
<p>i</p>
<p>a</p>
<p>int</p>
<p>able</p>
<p>inter</p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
样例输出
<small>Sample Output</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>3</p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
数据范围及提示
<small>Data Size & Hint</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p><span>1&lt;=N&lt;=10</span><sup>5</sup></p>
