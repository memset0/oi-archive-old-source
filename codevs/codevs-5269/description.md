题目描述
<small>Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

<p><span style="">给定平面上的</span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman';">n</span><span style="">个点，定义</span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman';">(x1,y1)</span><span style="">到</span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman';">(x2,y2)</span><span style="">的费用为</span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman';">min(|x1-x2|,|y1-y2|)</span><span style="">，求从</span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman';">1</span><span style="">号点走到</span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman';">n</span><span style="">号点的最小费用</span></p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
输入描述
<small>Input Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p><span style=""><span style="">第一行包含一个正整数</span>n<span style="">，表示点数。</span></span></p><p><span style=""><span style="">接下来</span>n<span style="">行，每行包含两个整数</span>x[i],y[i](0&lt;=x[i],y[i]&lt;=10^9)<span style="">，依次表示每个点的坐标。</span></span></p><p><br></p>

</div>
</div>
<div  class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
输出描述
<small>Output Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

<p><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 14px;">一个整数，即最小费用。</span></p><p><br/></p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="area-title">
