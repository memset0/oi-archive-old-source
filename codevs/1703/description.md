题目描述
<small>Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

<p><strong><span style="font-family: Courier New;">我们知道一个数字<span>m, </span>它既可以是<span>a</span>的<span>b</span>次方<span>, </span>也可以是<span>c</span>的<span>d</span>次方<span>. </span>不难看出<span>, a</span>和<span>c</span>之间应该是<span>(a, c)</span>≠<span>1. </span>现在给你<span>m</span>和<span>a, </span>请你计算出<span>b</span>来<span>.</span></span></strong></p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>

<small>Input Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

</div>
</div>
<div  class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>

<p><strong>共n行.&nbsp;每行为一个小数点后三位实数b,&nbsp;这个b满足a的b次方为m.</strong></p>

</div>
</div>
