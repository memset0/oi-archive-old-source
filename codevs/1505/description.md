<span>
题目描述
<small>Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

<p>有一篇“杂文”，由一些整数、带小数点的实数和乱七八糟的单词（字符串）组成。整数的范围必须在<span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman';">-70000</span><span style="">到</span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman';">70000</span><span style="">之间；实数用一般形式表示，而不用科学计数法，大小也在</span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman';">-70000</span><span style="">到</span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman';">70000</span><span style="">之间，（输入文件中保证）有效数字不超过</span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman';">10</span><span style="">位；凡不符合以上格式和范围的都是单词，一个单词内部不含空格。</span></p>
<p>这些内容由空格或者回车分割，连续的空格可能不只一个，也可能有空行。</p>
<p>求除了单词以外的整数和实数的算术平均值。</p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
输入描述
<small>Input Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>由整数、实数和单词间隔形成的文章。每行至多<span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman';">255</span><span style="">个字符。文章大小至多</span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman';">100KB</span><span style="">。</span></p>

</div>
</div>
<div  class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
输出描述
<small>Output Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

<p class="p0">文中符合要求的整数和实数的平均值，四舍五入保留小数点后<span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman';">2</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">位。</span></p>

</div>
</div>


<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
样例输入
<small>Sample Input</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>3 3.14 aaa  5a</p>
<p>A5 -3</p>
<p> </p>
<p>-3.14  5</p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
样例输出
<small>Sample Output</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>1.00</p>
