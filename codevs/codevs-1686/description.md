<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
题目描述
<small>Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

<p>图书馆按顺序排列有<span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman';">N</span><span style="">本书需要维护，每本书的总页数不相同。现有</span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman';">M</span><span style="">位员工。可以给每个员工分配连续的一段书籍，让他进行维护。现在的问题是，怎么样分配，工作任务最重（需要维护的页数最多）的人维护的页数尽量少。 </span></p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
输入描述
<small>Input Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>第一行两个数，<span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman';">N</span><span style="">、</span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman';">M</span><span style="">。接下来</span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman';">N</span><span style="">行，每行一个整数，表示一本书的页数。</span></p>

</div>
</div>
<div  class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
输出描述
<small>Output Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

<p class="p0">任务最重的人最少需要维护的页数。</p>

</div>
</div>


<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
样例输入
<small>Sample Input</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>5 3</p>
<p>3</p>
<p>2</p>
<p>4</p>
<p>1</p>
<p>5</p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
样例输出
<small>Sample Output</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>5</p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
数据范围及提示
<small>Data Size & Hint</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>数据范围：<span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman';">N&lt;=10</span>5，<span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman';">M&lt;=N</span><span style="">。一本书的页数最多</span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman';">10</span>4。时间限制为<span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman';">1s</span><span style="">。</span></p>
</div>
