<span>
题目描述
<small>Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

<p><span style="">有 </span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman',serif;">N </span><span style="">个二维坐标上的整数点 </span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman',serif;">(Xi,Yi) </span><span style="">。现在请你选择一个可以与 </span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman',serif;">N </span><span style="">个点重合的整数点 </span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman',serif;">(X,Y) 到 N 个点距离总和最小。</span></p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
输入描述
<small>Input Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p><span style=""></span></p><p><span style="">第一行一个整数 </span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman',serif;">N</span><span style="">。</span></p><p><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman',serif;"></span><span style="">接下来 </span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman',serif;">N </span><span style="">行，每行两个整数 </span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman',serif;">Xi</span><span style="">，</span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman',serif;">Yi</span><span style="">。</span></p>

</div>
</div>
<div  class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
输出描述
<small>Output Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

<p><span style="font-size: 14px; font-family: 宋体;">两个整数。最小距离和可行位置个数。</span></p>

</div>
</div>


<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
样例输入
<small>Sample Input</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p style=""><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;">4</span></p><p style=""><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;">1 -3</span></p><p style=""><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;">0 1</span></p><p style=""><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;">-2 1</span></p><p style=""><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;">1 -1</span></p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
样例输出
<small>Sample Output</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman',serif;">10  6</span></p>

</div>
</div>

<p><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman',serif;">2&lt;=N&lt;=10000</span><span style="">；其他数字绝对值小于等于 </span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman',serif;">10000</span><span style="">。</span></p>
