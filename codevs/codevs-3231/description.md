题目描述
<small>Description</small>

<p>这里简直是吃货的天堂，小吃太多了。数不胜数。</p>
<p>假设岛上有N种小吃，每种体积Vi，美味度Wi。</p>
<p>设某吃货旅行者的食量C,求他能吃到食品的最大美味度。</p>
<p> </p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>

<small>Input Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>两个正整数N  C</p>
<p>N个正整数，Vi</p>
<p>N个正整数，Wi</p>

</div>
</div>
<div  class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
输出描述
<small>Output Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

<p>最大美味值</p>

</div>
</div>


<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>

<p>10 24 1 100</p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
