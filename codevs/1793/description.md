<span>
题目描述
<small>Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

<p>‘<span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman';">DOS</span><span style="">’被称为良序字串，因为它之中的字母是按</span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman';">ASCII</span><span style="">码的顺序排列的，而‘</span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman';">DATE</span><span style="">’则不是良序字串。</span></p>
<p>现要求给出一个<span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman';">N*N</span><span style="">的字母方阵，从中选出给定长度</span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman';">M</span><span style="">的良序字串。</span></p>
<p><span style="">注明：良序字串的每个字母必须在同一行中连续。</span></p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
输入描述
<small>Input Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>第一行为N,M</p>
<p>接下来N行为N*N 的方阵</p>

</div>
</div>
<div  class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
输出描述
<small>Output Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

<p><span>输出给定长度</span><span>M</span><span>的良序字串</span></p>
<p><span>每行一个。</span></p>
<p><span><br /></span></p>

</div>
</div>


<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
样例输入
<small>Sample Input</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>4 3（<span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman';">N</span><span style="">和</span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman';">M</span><span style="">的值）</span></p>
<p>ABDE</p>
<p>CDDE</p>
<p>DEFK</p>
<p>CDKF</p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
样例输出
<small>Sample Output</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<div>
<p>ABD</p>
<p>BDE</p>
<p>DEF</p>
<p>EFK</p>
<p>CDK</p>
</div>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
数据范围及提示
<small>Data Size & Hint</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>N,M&lt;=5</p>
</div>
