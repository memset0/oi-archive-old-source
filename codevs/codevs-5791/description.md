题目描述
<small>Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

<p style=""><span style=""><span style="">有一本</span>n<span style="">个单词的字典，其中每个单词的长度不超过</span><span style="font-family: Calibri;">4</span><span style="">且大于</span><span style="font-family: Calibri;">0</span><span style="">。现在给你一篇文章，文章中没有分隔符，只有小写字母。现在需要你修改最少的字母，使文章是由字典中的单词构成。</span></span></p><p><br></p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
输入描述
<small>Input Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p style=""><span style=""><span style="">第一行一个数</span>n<span style="">。</span></span></p><p style=""><span style=""><span style="">接下来</span>n<span style="">行，每行一个单词。（单词可能有重复）</span></span></p><p style=""><span style=""><span style="">接下来一行一个数</span>m<span style="">，表示文章中字母的个数。</span></span></p><p style=""><span style=""><span style="">再接下来一行</span>m<span style="">个字母，表示文章。</span></span></p><p><br></p>

</div>
</div>
<div  class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
输出描述
<small>Output Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

<p style="text-indent:28px"><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px"><span style="font-family:宋体">仅一个数，表示最少需要修改的字母个数，若不能满足条件，则输出</span></span><span style=";font-family:Calibri;font-size:14px">’</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px">No</span><span style=";font-family:Calibri;font-size:14px">’</span><span style=";font-family:宋体;font-size:14px"><span style="font-family:宋体">（不包括引号）。</span></span></p><p><br/></p>

</div>
</div>
