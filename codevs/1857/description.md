题目描述
<small>Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

<p><span>一次考试共有</span><span>n</span><span>个人参加，第</span><span>i</span><span>个人说：“有</span><span>ai</span><span>个人分数比我高，</span><span>bi</span><span>个人分数比我低。”问最少有几个人没有说真话</span><span>(</span><span>可能有相同的分数</span><span>)</span></p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
输入描述
<small>Input Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p><span>第一行一个整数</span><span>n</span><span>，接下来</span><span>n</span><span>行每行两个整数，第</span><span>i+1</span><span>行的两个整数分别代表</span><span>ai</span><span>、</span><span>bi</span></p>

</div>
</div>
<div  class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
输出描述
<small>Output Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

<p><span>一个整数，表示最少有几个人说谎</span></p>

</div>
</div>


<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
样例输入
<small>Sample Input</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p><span>3</span><br><br><span> 2 0</span><br><br><span> 0 2</span><br><br><span> 2 2</span><br><br><br></p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
样例输出
<small>Sample Output</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p><span>1</span></p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>

<small>Data Size & Hint</small>
</span></div>
