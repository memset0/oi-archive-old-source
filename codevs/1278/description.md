<span>
题目描述
<small>Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

<p><span style="">给出一个有n位有效数字的小数r。r所在区间是[0,1).</span></p>
<p><span style="">让你求一个分母最小的分数,该分数化成小数四舍五入保留n位有效数字后为r。你只要输出分母的大小即可。</span></p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
输入描述
<small>Input Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p><span style="">输入含有若干行,每行一个整数n和一个含有n位有效数字的小数r。</span></p>

</div>
</div>
<div  class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
输出描述
<small>Output Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

<p><span style="font-size: medium;">输出若干行，每行一个解，表示最小的分母。</span></p>

</div>
</div>


<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
样例输入
<small>Sample Input</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p><span style="">2 0.33<br>3 0.316</span></p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
样例输出
<small>Sample Output</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p><span style="">3</span><br><span style="">19</span></p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
数据范围及提示
<small>Data Size & Hint</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>数据范围 1&lt;=n&lt;=15   0&lt;=r&lt;1</p>
<p> </p>
<p>样例说明 1/3 化成小数保留2位小数位 0.33</p>
<p> </p>
</div>
