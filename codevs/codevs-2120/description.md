<div class="panel panel-default">

<span>
题目描述
<small>Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

<p>小包有N个球和A个盒子。球标号为0到N-1，盒子编号为0到A-1。标号为x的球放在下标为x mod A的盒子里。</p>
<p>然后他得到了B个新盒子，编号为0到B-1。他想把所有球从旧盒子里面拿出来放到新盒子，标号为x的球放在下标为x mod B的盒子里。</p>
<p>对于某个球来说，如果原本放在下标为a的旧盒子，现在放在下表为b的新盒子，那么费用为|a-b|。</p>
<p>求出总花费。</p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
输入描述
<small>Input Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>输入3个整数N，A和B，同一行。</p>

</div>
</div>
<div  class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
输出描述
<small>Output Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

<p>输出总花费。</p>

</div>
</div>


<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
样例输入
<small>Sample Input</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p><span style="">8 2 4</span></p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
样例输出
<small>Sample Output</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p><span style="">8</span></p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="area-title">

<small>Data Size & Hint</small>
</span></div>

<p>对于全部数据，有1≤<em>N</em>≤1000000000，A,B≤100000<strong></strong></p>
