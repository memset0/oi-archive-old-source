<span>
题目描述
<small>Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

<p><span style="font-family: 'times new roman';">【NOIP2001最大公约数和最小公倍数问题】的加强版。<br><em>T</em>组数据，每组数据给出<em>d</em>，<em>m</em>，求满足gcd(<em>i</em>, <em>j</em>)=<em>d</em>且lcm(<em>i</em>, <em>j</em>)=<em>m</em>的(<em>i</em>, <em>j</em>)的个数。</span></p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
输入描述
<small>Input Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p><span style="font-family: 'times new roman';">第1行是一个数<em>T</em>，表示数据组数。<br>接下来的<em>T</em>行，每行两个数<em>d</em>，<em>m</em>，意义见题目描述。</span></p>

</div>
</div>
<div  class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
输出描述
<small>Output Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

<p><span style="font-family: &#39;times new roman&#39;;">输出应有<em>T</em>行，每行一个整数，表示该问题的答案。</span></p>

</div>
</div>


<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
样例输入
<small>Sample Input</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p><span style="font-family: 'times new roman';">1<br>3 60</span></p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
样例输出
<small>Sample Output</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p><span style="font-family: 'times new roman';">4</span></p><p><span style="font-family: 'times new roman';">提示：这4个(<em>i</em>, <em>j</em>)分别是(3, 60)，(12, 15)，(15, 12)，(60, 3)。</span></p>

</div>
</div>

<small>Data Size & Hint</small>
</span></div>
