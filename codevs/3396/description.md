<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
题目描述
<small>Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

<p>给出两个N*M的矩阵，请计算这两个矩阵对应位置元素相加后得到的新矩阵。</p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
输入描述
<small>Input Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>输入第一行包含两个正整数N和M。</p>
<p>接下来N行，每行M个数，描述第一个矩阵。</p>
<p>再接下来N行，每行M个数，描述第二个矩阵。</p>

</div>
</div>
<div  class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
输出描述
<small>Output Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

<p align="left">输出包括N行，每行M个数，描述相加后的新矩阵。</p>

</div>
</div>


<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
样例输入
<small>Sample Input</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>2 3</p>
<p>1 2 3</p>
<p>4 5 6</p>
<p>6 5 4</p>
<p>3 2 1</p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
样例输出
<small>Sample Output</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>7 7 7</p>
<p>7 7 7</p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
数据范围及提示
<small>Data Size & Hint</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>2≤M≤N≤100</p>
<p>保证矩阵中每个数小于32767。</p>
</div>
</div>
