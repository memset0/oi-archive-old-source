<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
题目描述
<small>Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

<p>小明正在玩一款《拯救水滴》的游戏。</p>
<p>游戏中有5*N的沙滩，上面有一些水滴，快要蒸发了。</p>
<p>你有一杯水，可以向任意一格滴，滴水可以连接不同片的水。</p>
<p>如果所有水滴都连成一片，水滴就可以流走，就活了。</p>
<p>救活它们用的水滴数就是游戏的得分。当然，得分越少越好。</p>
<p>求小明可得的最少得分。</p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
输入描述
<small>Input Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>第一行，整数N。</p>
<p>之后N行01串，每行0表示无水，1表示有水。</p>
<p> </p>

</div>
</div>
<div  class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
输出描述
<small>Output Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

<p>最少得分。</p>

</div>
</div>


<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
样例输入
<small>Sample Input</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>4</p>
<p>10101</p>
<p>11011</p>
<p>00110</p>
<p>10000</p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
样例输出
<small>Sample Output</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>2</p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
数据范围及提示
<small>Data Size & Hint</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>N&lt;=100..</p>
</div>
