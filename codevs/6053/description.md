<div class="area-title">
<span>
题目描述
<small>Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

<p><span style="">一个旅行者有一个最多能用V公斤的背包，现在有n件物品，它们的重量分别是W1，W2，...,Wn，它们的价值分别为C1,C2,...,Cn。这些物品被划分为若干组，每组中的物品互相冲突，最多选一件。求解将哪些物品装入背包可使这些物品的费用总和不超过背包容量，且价值总和最大。</span></p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
输入描述
<small>Input Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p><span style="">第一行：三个整数，V，N和T；</span></p><p><span style="font-family: Arial;"> 第2..N+1行：每行三个整数Wi,Ci,P，表示每个物品的重量，价值，所属组号。</span></p>

</div>
</div>
<div  class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
输出描述
<small>Output Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

<p><span style="font-size: 24px; font-family: 宋体;">仅一行，一个数，表示最大总价值。</span></p>

</div>
</div>


<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
样例输入
<small>Sample Input</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p><span style="font-family: Arial;">10 6 3</span> </p><p><span style="font-family: Arial;">2  1  1</span> </p><p><span style="font-family: Arial;">3  3  1</span> </p><p><span style="font-family: Arial;">4  8  2</span> </p><p><span style="font-family: Arial;">6  9  2</span> </p><p><span style="font-family: Arial;">2  8  3</span> </p><p><span style="font-family: Arial;">3  9  3</span></p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
样例输出
<small>Sample Output</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>20<br></p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
数据范围及提示
<small>Data Size & Hint</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>(背包容量，V&lt;=200)</p><p>(物品数量，N&lt;=30)</p><p>(最大组号，T&lt;=10)</p>
</div>
