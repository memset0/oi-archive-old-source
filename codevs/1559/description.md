<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
题目描述
<small>Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

<p>给定<span style="font-family: Times New Roman;">n</span><span style="">个各不相同的无序字母对（区分大小写，无序即字母对中的两个字母可以位置颠倒）。请构造一个有</span><span style="font-family: Times New Roman;">n+1</span><span style="">个字母的字符串使得每个字母对都在这个字符串中出现。</span></p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
输入描述
<small>Input Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>第一行输入一个正整数<span style="font-family: Times New Roman;">n</span><span style="">。</span></p>
<p>以下<span style="font-family: Times New Roman;">n</span><span style="">行每行两个字母，表示这两个字母需要相邻。</span></p>

</div>
</div>
<div  class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
输出描述
<small>Output Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

<p class="p0">输出满足要求的字符串。</p>
<p class="p0">如果没有满足要求的字符串，请输出<span style="font-family: Times New Roman;">&ldquo;No&nbsp;Solution&rdquo;</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">。</span></p>
<p class="p0">如果有多种方案，请输出前面的字母的<span style="font-family: Times New Roman;">ASCII</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">编码尽可能小的（字典序最小）的方案</span></p>

</div>
</div>


<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
样例输入
<small>Sample Input</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>4</p>
<p>aZ</p>
<p>tZ</p>
<p>Xt</p>
<p>aX</p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
样例输出
<small>Sample Output</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>XaZtX</p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="area-title">

<small>Data Size & Hint</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>【数据规模与约定】</p>
<p>不同的无序字母对个数有限，<span style="font-family: Times New Roman;">n</span><span style="">的规模可以通过计算得到。</span></p>
