<span>
题目描述
<small>Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

<p>输入10个由首字母为大写字母开头的名字，要求根据各个名字首字母由A到Z排序。（怎么样，很简单吧！）<br></p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
输入描述
<small>Input Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>10个由大写字母开头的名字。<br></p>

</div>
</div>
<div  class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
输出描述
<small>Output Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

<p>10行，每行先是序号（1-10）再是根据名字首字母（由A-Z）排序的姓名，中间用一个空格隔开,若有多个首字母相同的将按照输入顺序决定。<br/></p>

</div>
</div>


<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
样例输入
<small>Sample Input</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>Tom</p><p>Jerry<br></p><p>Merry</p><p>Helen</p><p>Jack</p><p>Anna</p><p>Ross</p><p>Julie</p><p>Gloria</p><p>Carol<br></p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
样例输出
<small>Sample Output</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>1 Anna<br>2 Carol<br>3 Gloria<br>4 Helen<br>5 Jerry<br>6 Jack<br>7 Julie<br>8 Merry<br>9 Ross<br>10 Tom<br></p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
数据范围及提示
<small>Data Size & Hint</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>每个名字长度≤20，共10个名字。</p><p>有的人可能想得太复杂了，其实没那么复杂。<br></p>
