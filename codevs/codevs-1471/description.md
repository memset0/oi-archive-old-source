<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
题目描述
<small>Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

<p>有N条线段，已知每条线段的起点和终点（50000以内），然后有M个询问，每次询问一个点（50000以内），求这个点在多少条线段上出现过？</p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
输入描述
<small>Input Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>第一行N线段条数<br>接下来N行，每行两个数，线段的起点和终点<br>第N+2行一个数M询问个数<br>接下来M行，每行一个点</p>

</div>
</div>
<div  class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
输出描述
<small>Output Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

<p>对于每个询问，求答案</p>

</div>
</div>


<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
样例输入
<small>Sample Input</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>3<br>2 5<br>4 6<br>0 7<br>4<br>2<br>4<br>7<br>6</p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
样例输出
<small>Sample Output</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>2<br>3<br>1<br>2</p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
数据范围及提示
<small>Data Size & Hint</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>N,M&lt;=50000</p>
</div>
</div>
