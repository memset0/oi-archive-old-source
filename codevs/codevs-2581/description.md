题目描述
<small>Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

<p>给出一个正整数x，问x最少能由多少个Fibonacci数加减算出。<br>例如1070=987+89-5-1，因此x=1070时答案是4。</p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
输入描述
<small>Input Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>第一行一个正整数q (q&lt;=10)，表示有q组输出。下面q行每行一个正整数x (x&lt;=4*10^17)。</p>

</div>
</div>
<div  class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
输出描述
<small>Output Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

<p>输出q行，依次表示每个输出的答案。</p>

</div>
</div>


<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
样例输入
<small>Sample Input</small>
</span></div>

<p>1<br>1070</p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
样例输出
<small>Sample Output</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>4</p>

</div>
