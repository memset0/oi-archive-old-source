题目描述
<small>Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

<p>现在有<span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman';">n</span><span style="">个物品（有可能相同），请您编程计算从中取</span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman';">k</span><span style="">个有多少种不同的取法。</span></p>

</div>
</div>

<span>

</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

</div>
</div>
<div  class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
输出描述
<small>Output Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

<p class="p0">输出仅一个整数，即方案数。</p>

</div>
</div>
