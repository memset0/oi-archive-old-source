<span>
题目描述
<small>Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

<p>给定一个数列{an}，这个数列满足ai<span style="">≠</span><span style="font-family: Verdana;">aj(i</span><span style="">≠</span><span style="font-family: Verdana;">j)</span>，现在要求你把这个数列从小到大排序，每次允许你交换其中任意一对数，请问最少需要几次交换？</p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
输入描述
<small>Input Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>第一行，正整数n (n&lt;=100,000)。</p>
<p>以下若干行，一共n个数，用空格分隔开，表示数列{an},任意-231&lt;ai&lt;231。</p>

</div>
</div>
<div  class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
输出描述
<small>Output Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

<p class="p0">只有一行，包含一个数，表示最少的交换次数。</p>

</div>
</div>


<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
样例输入
<small>Sample Input</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>8</p>
<p>8 23 4 16 77 -5 53 100</p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
样例输出
<small>Sample Output</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>5</p>

</div>
</div>
