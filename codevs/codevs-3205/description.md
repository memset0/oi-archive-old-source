题目描述
<small>Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

<p>两个连续质数p和q的差d，称为一个长度为d的质数间隙。例如23到29之间的质数间隙长度为6。输入正整数n，编程计算包含n的质数间隙长度。注意，当n为质数时，计算结果为0。</p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
输入描述
<small>Input Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>第一行是正整数t(≤1000)，表示数据组数；接下来的t行，每行一个正整数n(≤106)。</p>

</div>
</div>
<div  class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
输出描述
<small>Output Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

<p class="p0">对于每个n，输出包含n的质数间隙长度。</p>

</div>
</div>


<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
样例输入
<small>Sample Input</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>3</p>
<p>11</p>
<p>27</p>
<p>492170</p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
样例输出
<small>Sample Output</small>
</span></div>

<p>0</p>
<p>6</p>
<p>114</p>

</div>
</div>
