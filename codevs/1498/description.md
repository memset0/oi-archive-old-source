<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
题目描述
<small>Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

<p>现有一个<span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman';">n*m</span><span style="">的网格。你站在某个格子 </span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman';">(x, y) </span><span style="">中。</span></p>
<p>你可以选择向上、下、左、右四个方向走，但是已经到过的格子不能再被重复经过。</p>
<p>你的任务就是把所有的<span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman';">n*m</span><span style="">个格子不重复的遍历一遍。</span></p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
输入描述
<small>Input Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>第一行两个整数<span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman';">n, m</span><span style="">。（</span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman';">1&lt;=n, m&lt;=100</span><span style="">）</span></p>
<p>第二行两个整数<span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman';">x, y</span><span style="">。（</span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman';">1&lt;=x&lt;=n, 1&lt;=y&lt;=m</span><span style="">）</span></p>

</div>
</div>
<div  class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
输出描述
<small>Output Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

<p class="p0">如果对于给定的数据无解，则输出<span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman';">NO</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">；否则输出包括</span><span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman';">n*m</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;">行，每行两个整数代表一个坐标，这些坐标顺序代表你的遍历路线。</span></p>

</div>
</div>


<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
样例输入
<small>Sample Input</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>3 3</p>
<p>2 2</p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
样例输出
<small>Sample Output</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>2 2</p>
<p>2 3</p>
<p>1 3</p>
<p>1 2</p>
<p>1 1</p>
<p>2 1</p>
<p>3 1</p>
<p>3 2</p>
<p>3 3</p>

</div>
</div>
