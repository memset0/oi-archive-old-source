<span>
题目描述
<small>Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

<p><span>有n个数(n是奇数)，其中n-1个数两两成对，有1个数落单，找出这个数。要求O(n)的时间复杂度，O(1)的空间复杂度</span></p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
输入描述
<small>Input Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>第一行输入一个n， n是大于等于1的奇数</p>
<p>第二行包含n个整数</p>

</div>
</div>
<div  class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
输出描述
<small>Output Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

<p>输出那个落单的数</p>

</div>
</div>


<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
样例输入
<small>Sample Input</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>3</p>
<p>1 7 1</p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
样例输出
<small>Sample Output</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>7</p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
数据范围及提示
<small>Data Size & Hint</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>1&lt;=n&lt;=4000001  n是一个奇数</p>
</div>
