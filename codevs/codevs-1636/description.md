<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
题目描述
<small>Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

<p>有n个向量组成的式子，你要做的是将此式子化简到最简。</p>
<p> </p>
<p>注：零向量用”0“表示</p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
输入描述
<small>Input Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>第一行有一个整数n（0&lt;n&lt;=1000)</p>
<p>第2到n+1行依次是n个向量</p>

</div>
</div>
<div  class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
输出描述
<small>Output Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

<p>给出这n个向量的和，遇到无法化简成一个用输出数据的字母表示的向量并且答案不为&ldquo;0&rdquo;时，的情况时请输出&ldquo;Thompson Chelsea sitting on the tree"；可以化简成一个用输出数据的字母表示的向量时，数据保证有一个解。如果最后为零向量，输出0.</p>

</div>
</div>


<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
样例输入
<small>Sample Input</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>Sample 1:</p>
<p><span style="">2</span></p>
<p><span style="">AB</span></p>
<p>BC</p>
<p> </p>
<p>Sample 2:</p>
<p>2</p>
<p>AB</p>
<p>AB</p>
<p> </p>
<p>Sample 3:</p>
<p>2</p>
<p>AB</p>
<p>FG</p>
<p> </p>
<p>Sample 4:</p>
<p>1</p>
<p>0</p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
样例输出
<small>Sample Output</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>Sample 1:</p>
<p>AC</p>
<p> </p>
<p>Sample 2:</p>
<p>2AB</p>
<p> </p>
<p>Sample 3:</p>
<p>Thompson Chelsea sitting on the tree</p>
<p> </p>
<p>Sample 4:</p>
<p>0</p>
<p> </p>

</div>
</div>
