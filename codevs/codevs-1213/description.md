<span>
题目描述
<small>Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

<p>已知整数<em>x</em>,<em>y</em>满足如下面的条件:</p>
<p> </p>
<p>ax+by+c = 0</p>
<p>p&lt;=x&lt;=q</p>
<p>r&lt;=y&lt;=s</p>
<p> </p>
<p>求满足这些条件的<em>x</em>,<em>y</em>的个数。</p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
输入描述
<small>Input Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>第一行有一个整数<em>n</em>（<em>n</em>&lt;=10），表示有n个任务。n&lt;=10</p>
<p>以下有<em>n</em>行，每行有7个整数，分别为：<em>a</em>,<em>b</em>,<em>c</em>,<em>p</em>,<em>q</em>,<em>r</em>,<em>s</em>。均不超过10<sup>8</sup>。</p>

</div>
</div>
<div  class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
输出描述
<small>Output Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

<p>共<em>n</em>行，第<em>i</em>行是第<em>i</em>个任务的解的个数。</p>

</div>
</div>


<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
样例输入
<small>Sample Input</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>2</p>
<p>2 3 -7 0 10 0 10</p>
<p>1 1 1 -10 10 -9 9</p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
样例输出
<small>Sample Output</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>1</p>
<p>19</p>

</div>
</div>
