<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
题目描述
<small>Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

<p>在一个字符串中，把<strong>相同</strong>的字符<strong>两两</strong>删除，求剩下字符的个数。</p>
<p>例如：字符串<strong style="">asasda，</strong></p>
<p>首先删除两个'<strong>a</strong>'，得到字符串'<strong>ssda</strong>'。</p>
<p>然后删除两个'<strong>s</strong>'，得到字符串'<strong>da</strong>'。</p>
<p>此时，字符串中没有了相同的字符，删除结束，剩下个数为2。</p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
输入描述
<small>Input Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>一个字符串，含义如描述所示。</p>

</div>
</div>
<div  class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
输出描述
<small>Output Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

<p>字符串中剩余字符的个数。</p>

</div>
</div>


<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
样例输入
<small>Sample Input</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>asasda</p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
样例输出
<small>Sample Output</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>2</p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
数据范围及提示
<small>Data Size & Hint</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p>字符串的长度&lt;=1,000,000（用<strong>ansistring</strong>）。</p>
<p>字符串中只会包含小写字母。</p>
<p>只要求输出剩余的个数。</p>
</div>
</div>
