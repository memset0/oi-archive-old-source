<span>
题目描述
<small>Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

<p><span>　　现在我们的主角正在玩一个游戏:</span><br><span>　　给出一个普通无向图,判断它是否是平面图.</span><br><span>　　在主角光环的照耀下,他成功的玩过了10000个点的那一关(0条边)</span><br><span>　　明白一个苹果两人分这个道理的他决定把这个游戏分享给你,一起来玩吧!</span><br><span>　　一个输入数据有2组,都对才有分.</span></p>
<p><span><br></span></p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
输入描述
<small>Input Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p><span>　　对于每一组:</span><br><span>　　第一行n,m,n个点,m条边</span><br><span>　　下面m行,x,y表示&lt;x,y&gt;这条边</span></p>

</div>
</div>
<div  class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
输出描述
<small>Output Description</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">

<p><span>　　对于每一组:</span><br /><span>　　yes 或者 no,表示可否成为平面图.</span></p>

</div>
</div>


<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
样例输入
<small>Sample Input</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p><span>5 9</span><br><span>1 2</span><br><span>1 3</span><br><span>1 4</span><br><span>1 5</span><br><span>2 3</span><br><span>2 4</span><br><span>2 5</span><br><span>3 5</span><br><span>4 5</span><br><span>10000 0</span></p>

</div>
</div>

<div class="panel panel-default">
<div class="area-title">
<span>
样例输出
<small>Sample Output</small>
</span></div>
<div class="panel-body">
<p><span>yes</span><br><span>yes</span></p>

</div>
</div>
